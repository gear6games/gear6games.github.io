🪸 Privacy Policy

Jelly Grab  ·  Gear 6 Games  ·  Effective: April 7, 2026

Overview

Jelly Grab ("the Game") is developed and published by Gear 6 Games ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you play the Game, how it is used, and your choices regarding that information.

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any information that directly identifies you as an individual.

Information Collected Automatically

When you play Jelly Grab, the following information may be collected automatically by third-party services we use:

  • Device information — device model, operating system version, screen resolution
  • Gameplay data — levels played, level completion, time spent, in-game actions
  • App performance data — crashes, errors, app start/close events
  • Advertising data — ad impressions, ad clicks, ad interaction data
  • Anonymous player ID — a randomly generated ID used to save your progress (not linked to your identity)

In-App Purchases

Jelly Grab offers optional in-app purchases including animal unlocks, the VIP Pass, Ad Free Pass, and Angel Mermaid PowerUp charges. All purchases are processed securely through Google Play Billing. We do not store your payment card details.

Purchases are tied to your Google Play account. Refund requests are subject to Google Play's refund policy.

Advertising

Jelly Grab displays banner advertisements and optional rewarded advertisements (which you choose to watch in exchange for in-game bonuses). Ads are served by Google AdMob.

Players who purchase the Ad Free Pass or VIP Pass will not see banner advertisements. Rewarded ads remain available as a voluntary option for all players.

To opt out of personalised advertising on Android, go to Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation.

Children's Privacy

Jelly Grab is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided personal information through the Game, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Data Retention

Game progress data stored via Unity Cloud Save is retained for as long as you have the Game installed or until you request deletion. Analytics data collected by Firebase and AdMob is retained according to their respective privacy policies.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the data collected about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of personalised advertising
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, or to request deletion of your game data, please contact us at the email address below.
